About AlphaRank and
Customer Influence

We reveal hidden customer connections and purchase influence between them.

AlphaRank is a behavioral science company that studies how groups of people make decisions. More specifically, we study how people make purchase decisions. Using sophisticated machine learning, we reveal hidden customer connections and purchase influence between them.

AlphaRank uses a network diffusion algorithm that finds a realistic social network consistent with the observed purchasing patterns in the network if they were made in the context of social influence ­while accounting for the fact that some decisions may not involve social influence.

The AlphaRank platform is a future proof, high­ performance, distributed computing platform capable of massive data interpretation with high rate of training­ testing cycles. It is highly-tailorable for different verticals.

Not all customers are created equal. Thanks to advances in technology and instant connectivity, some customers have far greater influence than others with very real potential impacts to the bottom line.

Until now, there's been no reliable way to pinpoint how an individual customer's purchase, behavior or social sentiment is going to impact the future purchases of other customers. That all changes with AlphaRank.

Thanks to our cutting edge analytics, easy integration and immediate and actionable results, your company can now easily identify, engage and maximize your most valuable, influential customers to improve ROI of marketing initiatives, accelerate sales and capture market share.
